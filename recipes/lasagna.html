Lasagna

Home

Lasagna is a hearty Italian dish made by layering flat pasta sheets with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and gooey melted mozzarella. It's the ultimate comfort food - baked until bubbling and golden brown, perfect for feeding a crowd or saving as leftovers. Each bite delivers a perfect balanced of savory, cheesy, and saucy goodness.

Ingredients

For the meat sauce:

  • 1 lb ground beef (or Italian sausage)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2–3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (24 oz) jar of marinara sauce (or homemade)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning

For the cheese layer:

  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Other:

  • 9 lasagna noodles (uncooked or oven-ready)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil (for sautéing)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the noodles (if not using oven-ready): Boil in salted water until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  3. Make the meat sauce:
    • In a large pan, heat a bit of olive oil.
    • Add chopped onions and cook until soft.
    • Add garlic and sauté briefly.
    • Add ground beef, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned.
    • Stir in marinara sauce and Italian seasoning. Let simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  4. Prepare the cheese filling:
    • In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan, parsley (optional), and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  5. Assemble the lasagna in a 9x13 baking dish:
    • Spread a layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
    • Place 3 lasagna noodles on top.
    • Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
    • Sprinkle mozzarella over the ricotta.
    • Repeat layers: sauce → noodles → ricotta → mozzarella, ending with sauce and mozzarella on top.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  7. Remove foil and bake for another 15–20 minutes, until the top is bubbly and golden.
  8. Let it rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing. This helps it set and hold its shape.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
